# California Revenue and Taxation Code Section 17043

Source: https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=17043.&lawCode=RTC

## Mental Health Services Tax

(a) For each taxable year beginning on or after January 1, 2005, in addition to any other taxes imposed by this part, an additional tax shall be imposed at the rate of 1 percent on that portion of a taxpayer's taxable income in excess of one million dollars ($1,000,000).

(b) For purposes of applying Part 10.2 (commencing with Section 18401) of Division 2, the tax imposed under this section shall be treated as if imposed under Section 17041.

(c) Notwithstanding any other provision of this part, the following shall not apply to the tax imposed under this section:

(1) Section 17039, relating to allowance of credits.

(2) Section 17041, relating to filing status and recomputation of the income tax brackets.

(3) Section 17045, relating to joint returns.

---

**Legislative History:**
- Added by Proposition 63, approved November 2, 2004
- Operative January 1, 2005

**Note:** The Mental Health Services Tax creates an additional 1% tax on taxable income exceeding $1,000,000. Combined with the top marginal rate of 12.3% (as indexed), this results in an effective top marginal rate of 13.3% for high-income taxpayers.
